01 Project Objective

The objective of this project was to use SQL to answer key business questions relating to revenue performance, customer behaviour, product performance, and regional sales trends. The analysis was performed using Microsoft's AdventureWorks Data Warehouse and demonstrates how data can be transformed into actionable business insights.

Tools & Skills Demonstrated

SQL Server T-SQL CTEs Window Functions Data Analysis Customer Segmentation Business Reporting

02 Business Questions Answered

  • How have online sales changed year-over-year?
  • Which product categories generate the most revenue?
  • Which customer segments contribute the most revenue?
  • Which regions perform best?
  • How much revenue comes from repeat customers?

03 Year-over-Year Revenue Analysis

This analysis evaluates overall online sales performance and identifies whether revenue is increasing or declining over time.

Business Question
What are total online sales and how do they compare with the previous year?
Insight
Year-over-year revenue analysis provides a high-level view of business growth and is often one of the first metrics reviewed by management teams.

04 Product Category Performance

Revenue was analysed by product category to identify the company's strongest revenue generators.

Business Insight
Understanding which product categories contribute most to total revenue supports inventory planning, marketing investment, and pricing strategies.

05 Customer Segmentation Analysis

Customers were grouped by purchasing behaviour to determine which segments deliver the greatest revenue contribution.

Frequency Group Lifecycle Stage Value Tier
Business Insight
Identifying high-value customer segments enables targeted retention and marketing initiatives.

06 Geographic Sales Performance

Revenue was analysed by geographic region to identify the strongest performing markets.

Business Insight
Regional analysis highlights which markets are performing well and where growth opportunities may exist.

07 Repeat Customer Revenue Analysis

Customer retention was analysed by comparing repeat purchasers with one-time buyers.

Business Insight
A high percentage of repeat customer revenue is often a sign of customer satisfaction and long-term business sustainability.

08 Key Recommendations

→
Focus investment on high-performing product categories.
→
Retain high-value customer segments through loyalty initiatives.
→
Explore growth opportunities in underperforming regions.
→
Monitor revenue trends through automated reporting.
→
Use customer segmentation to support targeted marketing.

09 Project Summary

This project demonstrates how SQL can be used to answer real business questions using Microsoft's AdventureWorks data warehouse. The analysis explored sales performance, customer behaviour, product performance, regional trends, and customer retention, providing insights that could support operational and strategic decision-making.
